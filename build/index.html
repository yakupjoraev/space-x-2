Rules

To be eligible for the NFT giveaway, participants must have a balance on their wallet that is equal to or greater than the cost of the NFT being given away.

This rule can help prevent fraudulent activity, such as creating multiple accounts to increase the chances of winning, as each account would need to have the required balance. This helps to ensure fairness in the giveaway and reduces the likelihood of abuse.
